Community Support

At OpenWISP, we are committed to providing excellent support to our users. Whether you have questions, need assistance, or want to contribute, there are several channels available to connect with the community.

Mailing List

Our mailing list is a great place to discuss ideas, and ask questions to the most active users and contributors.

Mailing List

Discussions

The Github Discussions Forum is an alternative to the mailing list for those who prefer forum style discussions.

Discussions

General Chat

For those who want to connect with other community members and chat in real time, this is the ideal space to do it.

General Chat

Development Chat

Developers and contributors coordinate here. While we strive to respond promptly, please note that response times may vary due to different time zones.

Dev Chat

Google Summer of Code

For those considering to participate in the Google Summer of Code with OpenWISP, we have a dedicated mailing list packed with valuable information.

GSoC Mailing List

Reporting Security Issues

If you discover a security vulnerability in OpenWISP, please report it promptly to security@openwisp.io. We appreciate your cooperation in keeping our community safe.

Security Report

For more information on how to get involved, contribute, or seek assistance, please explore our documentation or reach out to us via the channels mentioned above.
We look forward to hearing from you and collaborating together to make OpenWISP even better!
