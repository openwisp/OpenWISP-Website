Commercial Support Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

  • Can you train and support us to implement our own OpenWISP customizations?

    We appreciate your interest in customizing OpenWISP, but our commercial support service does not include code training.

    While we provide comprehensive assistance and guidance for using OpenWISP and troubleshooting issues, we are not an organization specialized in providing code training. Our support focuses on resolving technical issues, optimizing system performance, and ensuring the effective deployment of OpenWISP within your infrastructure.

    We also cannot offer support for OpenWISP extensions developed by third parties. If you choose to implement customizations independently, it becomes challenging for us to provide assistance, and we prefer to refrain from assuming any responsibility in such cases, as we don't have control over the code being written.

  • Can you deploy the first OpenWISP instance for us and train us to replicate the same setup for other customers?

    We appreciate your interest in leveraging our expertise to deploy OpenWISP instances and replicate our setup for other customers. However, we regret to inform you that we are unable to provide this service.

    As part of our commitment to maintaining the quality and integrity of our commercial support service, we have developed proprietary tools and processes that enable us to fine-tune and automate our support offerings. These tools are integral to our operations and constitute valuable trade secrets that we are unable to disclose or share with other organizations.

    While we are more than willing to assist you with deploying your initial OpenWISP instance and providing guidance on its configuration and usage, we are unable to offer training to develop services which could potentially compete with our own commercial support service.

  • Will you be able to issue us an invoice?

    Yes, openwisp OÜ is a company registered under the laws of the Republic of Estonia and can issue official invoices for any services or products purchased. Please provide the necessary billing details, and we'll ensure you receive an invoice in compliance with standard accounting practices.

Pricing Questions

  • What currencies do you accept?

    We accept payments in Euro (€) and US Dollar ($).

  • Can you give us a discount?

    As an open-source organization, we already provide a lot for free, including access to our code, community support, and extensive documentation. Maintaining and developing OpenWISP requires significant resources, and while we aim to be accessible to everyone, we rely on revenue from services to sustain ongoing development. Unfortunately, this means we can't offer additional discounts beyond what is already available to help fund the continued growth and improvement of the project.
