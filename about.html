Who is involved in the OpenWISP project?

Board of Directors


Maurizio Goretti
Has been a board member and NaMeX's CEO since 2000. His current research interests include: Inter-ISP networking, Internet Exchange Point, Datacenter and Wi-Fi architectures and security.

Davide Guerri
Designed and developed the first public release of the OpenWISP platform in 2008. Former CTO of the OpenWISP project, he has been working for HP in Bristol, UK since October 2013.

Francesco Loriga
Electronic engineer. Keen on networks, internet and openness. He believes to be the project Open Source Strategist although no one has appointed him as such. Wannabe rebetico musician.

Core Development Team


Federico Capoano
in the last few years has been actively involved in wireless community networks; he is a hybrid between a developer and a designer. His work is driven by passion for the ICT and by his desire of constant self-improvement.

Ilaria de Marinis
is working at CASPUR and has been a member of the Technical Scientific Committee of IDEM since 2007. Her interests lie in Ruby on Rails and in the OpenWisp Captive Portal.

Andrea Ferraresi
is working at CASPUR on the OpenWISP Firmware, his efforts are now mainly focused on testing and developing an integrated solution for AP's configuration retrieval.

Matteo Latini
even though he sometimes wear the sysadmin's hat, Matteo mainly works on OpenWISP as a web developer. He's currently enjoying Ruby on Rails, jQuery and HTML5.
